Where We Belong

Welcome to SHAKTI, A team solely devoted to empower women in science and technology. A heartfelt creation by SPIE Techno India student chapter, part of SPIE, The Internationl Society for Optics and Photonics.

More

Who We Are

We are a group of students who wants to envision a society where females are no way weaker than males, in terms of achieveing quality education and contributing to the society. We support academia, the peaceful environment with equal rights. Ultimately, we believe in our potential to spread the positiveness.

Next

What We Do

We conduct outreach programs, help students in need, held seminars and panel discussions, make physical and virtual products that can spur "scientific minds". In short, we try our best to prove that we exist and we support co-existence of males and females.
